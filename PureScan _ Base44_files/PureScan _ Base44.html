    Plan

    Intent & Goal

    SafeScan is a personal product safety checker app inspired by Oasis. It allows the user to scan or search any consumer product — food, skincare, hair care, household items, water bottles, and more — and instantly get a detailed safety analysis. The app surfaces harmful/toxic ingredients, allergens, and an overall health score so the user can make informed decisions about everything they bring into their life.

    Audience & Roles

    Single personal user (no multi-user or team features needed). The user is the sole account holder who scans products and views their personal history. No admin or guest roles required.

    Core Flows

    These flows must work end-to-end:

    User opens app → lands on a home screen with a prominent Scan button and a search bar → taps Scan to open camera and scans a product barcode OR types a product name/brand in search → app looks up the product via an AI/external data source

    Product result page is displayed showing: product name, image, category, overall safety score (A–F or 0–100 like Oasis), breakdown of harmful/toxic ingredients (e.g. parabens, sulfates, BPA, phthalates, formaldehyde, artificial dyes), allergen flags (nuts, gluten, fragrance, etc.), and a plain-English verdict ('This product is generally safe' / 'Use with caution' / 'Avoid')

    Scanned/searched product is automatically saved to the user's personal Scan History → user can view their history as a list of past products with their scores → tap any item to re-view its full safety report

    User can search their scan history by name or filter by safety rating to quickly find previously checked products

    Technical Requirements

    Use AI (GPT-style) to analyze product ingredients and generate safety scores, harmful ingredient flags, allergen detection, and plain-English verdicts. Barcode scanning via device camera (use a JS barcode scanning library). Product lookup via Open Food Facts API or similar open product database as a primary source, with AI fallback for products not in the database. Store scan history as a local entity per user session. Display ingredient analysis in structured card sections (Harmful Ingredients, Allergens, Overall Score).

    Design Preferences

    Modern consumer health app aesthetic — clean white background with bold accent colors (green for safe, amber for caution, red for avoid). Large readable typography, prominent safety score displayed as a circular gauge or letter grade. Card-based layout for ingredient breakdowns. Smooth, minimal UI with subtle rounded corners and shadow depth. Mobile-first, thumb-friendly design. No clutter — results should feel scannable at a glance.
